The Final Days

Arrest and Trial

Rizal was arrested in Manila on his return from exile. He was charged with sedition and rebellion, though he had denounced the revolution.

Execution

On December 30, 1896, Rizal was executed by firing squad at Bagumbayan (now Rizal Park). His last words were "Consummatum est" - "It is finished."

Legacy of Sacrifice

Rizal's execution was meant to quell the Philippine Revolution, but it had the opposite effect. His death galvanized the Filipino people and turned him into a martyr.

The revolution intensified, leading to Philippine independence. Rizal's sacrifice demonstrated the power of peaceful reform and the cost of colonial oppression.

Key Events in 1896: Rizal's execution. The outbreak of the Philippine Revolution. Rizal's transformation from reformer to national hero and martyr.
